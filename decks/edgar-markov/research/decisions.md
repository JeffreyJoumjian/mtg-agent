EDGAR MARKOV — DECISION LOG
============================================================
Live deck : decks/Edgar-D1ImpBudg_UPGRADED_2026-06-26.txt  (100 cards)
Identity  : go-wide vampire aristocrats + tokens. Win = flood vampire tokens ->
            aristocrats drain (Blood Artist/Cruel Celebrant/Sanctum Seeker/Cordial)
            + combat, with the DRAIN COMBO (Exquisite Blood + Sanguine Bond/Vito) as
            a backup kill. BRACKET 3 (0 Game Changers).
Companion files: cards.txt (card cache), UPGRADE-ROADMAP.txt (what's done / to buy).

=== CORE BUILD DECISIONS =====================================
- COMBOS: keep the DRAIN combo (Exquisite Blood + Sanguine Bond/Vito + Bloodthirsty
  Conqueror redundancy). TRIMMED the Cathars combo -> CUT Animation Module,
  Pawn of Ulamog, Scion of Opulence.  KEPT Cathars' Crusade + Ashnod's Altar
  (both great standalone). Animation Module was a near-blank without Cathars.
- BOARD WIPES (3): The Meathook Massacre + Toxic Deluge + Olivia's Wrath.
  CUT Blasphemous Act (Meathook is a one-sided-ish wipe + drain finisher; pulled from
  Innistrad Remastered boxes). 3-4 wipes is correct for go-wide.
- TOKEN DOUBLER: ELSPETH, STORM SLAYER (chosen over Anointed Procession).
  Reason: ~$40 cheaper, survives our own -X/-X wipes (planeswalker), and does more
  (doubles + makes tokens + anthem/flying finisher + removal). Dropped Anointed.
- Akroma's Will: bought + KEPT (bonus protection/alpha-strike finisher; runs alongside
  Elspeth, different role).
- CARD DRAW: was our weakest stat (3 vs premium decks' 8-12). Added 6 vampires/engines:
  Champion of Dusk, Dusk Legion Zealot, Dusk Legion Duelist, Twilight Prophet,
  Preacher of the Schism, Idol of Oblivion  (now ~9 draw).
- Captivating Vampire: KEPT (do NOT cut). 3/3 premium decks run it; consensus lord.
  (Earlier plan to cut it for Twilight Prophet was REVERSED.)

=== "KEEP THE BUDGET VERSION" (NOT upgrade targets) =========
- Dictate of Erebos  > Grave Pact      -- Flash wins (surprise edict / dodge wipes).
- Unbreakable Formation > Flawless Maneuver -- Addendum (+1/+1 whole board + vigilance
  on your main phase) = a go-wide finisher, not just reactive protection.
- Generous Gift: KEEP. Premium catch-all (destroys ANY permanent incl. lands/planeswalkers
  /enchantments that Swords/Terminate can't touch). 3/3-token downside is minor.

=== CARDS CUT (for the 14 upgrades) + WHY ===================
- Rakish Heir          : weakest card (slow attack-only counter; 1/3 premium).
- Vicious Conquistador : budget filler (0/3 premium). -> became Dusk Legion Zealot.
                         NOTE: NOT upgraded to Charismatic Conqueror (slot went to draw).
- Indulgent Aristocrat : redundant sac outlet (had 6).
- Bloodletter of Aclazotz: was cut, then RE-ADDED (2026-06-28) over Idol of Oblivion.
  Reason to re-add: strong drain doubler + flying vampire body (eminence); triple-black
  {1}{B}{B}{B} is now castable with the upgraded black-heavy manabase; cutting Idol (a
  colorless, no-body artifact) rebalanced over-stocked draw (9->8) and kept all 4
  protection pieces. Idol was the cleanest cut (only non-vampire flex draw card).
- Animation Module / Pawn of Ulamog / Scion of Opulence : Cathars-combo support; Pawn also
  only triggers off NONtoken deaths (anti-synergy with our token plan).
- Rush of Battle: cut early (101->100 trim), never replaced.
- Boros Charm: cut in the PREMIUM-UPGRADE pass (the 2026-06-26 fork build), effectively
  FOR Akroma's Will. Akroma's Will does Boros Charm's jobs better + team-wide + free with
  commander (whole-team indestructible/lifelink OR a flying+double-strike alpha-strike kill).
  Boros Charm's indestructible mode was redundant and doesn't stop our own -X/-X wipes.
  Assessment: fine cut, NOT worth re-adding (protection suite is solid at 4).

=== CARDS UNDER CONSIDERATION (not yet in deck) =============
- Coat of Arms ({5} artifact, ~$19): QUEUED for the final build. Quadratic tribal anthem
  (+1/+1 per OTHER same-type creature) — on a wide vampire board it's a swing-for-lethal
  finisher; stacks with the lords + Shared Animosity + Elspeth's doubled tokens. Catches:
  (1) SYMMETRICAL — also pumps opponents' shared-type creatures (real risk vs other tribal/
  token decks); (2) it's a non-interactive WIN-MORE payoff, and payoffs aren't our weak spot
  (removal/wraths are). Verdict: strong FLEX/Pocket — great vs slow grindy pods, side out vs
  tribal mirrors. Must EARN a slot in the finalizer vs interaction; not an auto-include.

=== EVALUATED & PASSED (do not re-litigate) =================
- Dark Ritual ({B} instant, one-time BBB): NO. One-shot ritual = spend a card for +2 mana
  ONCE, then it's a dead draw. Only pays off in cEDH combo/storm that ends on an explosive
  turn. Our deck is Bracket-3 midrange grind that wants card advantage + repeatable mana;
  BBB doesn't even fix for Edgar's R/W. Great card, wrong deck.

=== MANABASE DECISIONS ======================================
- 13 basics + premium-leaning nonbasics. Target ~16-18 nonbasic / ~18-20 basic was the
  early goal; now leaning more premium.
- ONLY buy lands that make a MARDU pair (W/B, B/R, R/W). Skip anything that makes
  blue or green (off-color = dead).
- Land destruction is NOT a concern at Bracket 3 (mass land denial is discouraged); don't
  hoard basics for "wipe insurance."
- 6 premium Mardu duals targeted: Blood Crypt, Godless Shrine, Sacred Foundry, Spectator
  Seating (IN deck) + Luxury Suite, Vault of Champions (STILL TO BUY).

=== STRATEGY / META CALLS ===================================
- cEDH: this deck CANNOT compete (no blue = no free counters; too slow; combat-based).
  Even a fully-decked Edgar isn't cEDH. Great for BRACKET 3 / bracket-capped EDH events.
- Bracket: BRACKET 3 with 0 Game Changers. The Bracket-4 lever = the 3 Game-Changer
  upgrades (Smothering Tithe, Demonic Tutor, Vampiric Tutor). Buy only if pod wants it.
- Sealed product: ALWAYS buy SINGLES, never boxes/precons, for deck upgrades.
  Skipped: Double Masters 2022 box ($340, -EV), Marvel Commander precons.
- Box pulls obtained: Edgar Markov + The Meathook Massacre (Innistrad Remastered),
  Blazemire Verge (Duskmourn).
  Roaming Throne = only in Marvel's SPIDER-MAN boosters (NOT Marvel Super Heroes).
  Black Market Connections = only in Marvel COMMANDER precons (bought as single instead).
