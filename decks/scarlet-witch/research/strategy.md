# Scarlet Witch — Strategy

## Identity
**"Wanda, the greatest witch alive."** A resilient, reality-warping Izzet spellcaster. The deck
should *feel* like being Wanda — spells everywhere, bending the game, protecting herself — not a
checklist of Marvel names. Immersion first, but it still has to function.

## Commander
**Scarlet Witch, Chaotic Avenger** — {2}{U}{R}, Izzet (U/R), 3/3 flying Mutant Warlock Hero.
On combat damage to a player: impulse-cast a Hero or noncreature spell for free.

- Chosen over the mono-red Wanda cards **because she's the only Wanda card with blue**, which
  unlocks the reality-warping / time / counterspell toolbox the theme needs.
- **The Vision and Scarlet Witch** ({2}{R}{R}, grows + ramps off spells) drops into the 99 as a
  second protect-and-beef threat. (~$60 card → 💰 proxy candidate.)

## Power / budget
- **Bracket 3** (upgraded/focused): efficient staples, at most one combo-ish finish, no Game
  Changers or fast-mana degeneracy.
- **Mixed budget:** build the strong version; tag expensive cards with 💰 as proxy candidates
  (lands especially get proxied).

## Playstyle goal
Survive and outlast — the player is happy to place 2nd-and-alive over durdling into an early
death. So: **resilient, control-ish midrange** that protects its threats and closes when ready,
not a glass-cannon. Win = evasive grown-up threat (sudden unblockable swing) on top of a
survive-forever plan.

## Pillars (in priority order, given the survival goal)
1. **Protection & counters** — load-bearing. Counterspells + equipment/redirect to keep Wanda &
   Vision alive.
2. **Reality-warping** — answers + flavor. Chaos Warp, Curse of the Swine, clones, board-rewrites
   double as removal/resets.
3. **Spell-copy & summoning** — resilient board + payoff doublers. **Flavor rule: only "energy
   made flesh" tokens** — Elementals, Spirits, Illusions, Construct/energy. No random goblins/dragons.
4. **Extra turns** — a *light* value splash (a few Time Warp effects), never a loop. Keeps us in
   Bracket 3 and off the "table villain" list.
- **Spellcasting engine** is the baseline under all of it: cast cheap spells → ramp + grow threats
  → payoffs trigger.
- **Explosive finish (baked in):** a small unblockable package (Whispersilk Cloak, Rogue's Passage)
  so a grown threat can suddenly connect for a lot.

## Build philosophy
Build a strong, balanced baseline first, as **labeled modules** (protection / reality-warp / token
/ draw / ramp). Play it, then turn a module's knob up/down based on what the games reveal
(dying to creatures? can't find gas? run over early?). Ships tunable on purpose.

## Notes learned while researching
- "Witch" is **not** a creature type in MTG; the witch-flavored type is **Warlock** (Wanda's own
  cards are "Mutant Warlock Hero"). Search `t:warlock id<=ur` for witch-flavored creatures.
- The Scarlet-Witch-art **Chaos Warp** is the `mar #69` printing (Joe Quesada / House of M art),
  distinct from the `msc #164` Commander printing (different art).
- Magneto and Agatha Harkness are **not** in the Marvel Super Heroes set (held for a future X-Men set).
