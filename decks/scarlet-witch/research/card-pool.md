## Deckbuilding direction

Every mechanically-Wanda card is **Red or Izzet (U/R)** — build **mono-Red** or **Izzet**, never
Mardu/Selesnya/4-color.

1. **Mono-Red storm/spellslinger (most on-theme):** Commander = **The Scarlet Witch (MSH #151)**.
   Matches EDHREC's "Storming Off with The Scarlet Witch" shell. Support already in-family and mono-R:
   The Vision and Scarlet Witch, Wanda's Vision, Hex Magic, Grapeshot, Wonder Man, Quicksilver (Brash
   Blur), Wiccan, Speed, + Chaos Warp removal.
2. **Izzet (U/R) value/flyers:** Commander = **Scarlet Witch, Chaotic Avenger (MSC #93)**. Adds
   Quicksilver (Speedster), Vision (Synthezoid Avenger), card advantage + evasion over raw storm speed.

**Best physical starting point:** the **"Avengers Assemble" precon (Jeskai W/U/R)** — ships the most
Wanda-family cards together (Chaotic Avenger + Quicksilver Speedster + Vision Synthezoid + Speed).
Buy it, then rebuild toward mono-Red or Izzet, cutting the white cards.
